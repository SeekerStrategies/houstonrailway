Houston Must Be Heard

Some of Houston's most struggling communities are already dealing with the harmful environmental fallout of one railway merger. No more mergers affecting our community without our voices being heard.

Take Action

WHAT IS HAPPENING?

On September 15th, the nearest public meeting on the merger of the Canadian Pacific and Kansas City Southern Railways will take place in Beaumont. Houston communities demand better.

We need a hearing in Houston, where families directly impacted by the merger can be heard.

WHY DOES THIS MATTER

For years, the health, safety, and well-being of Houston’s poorest and most Black and Brown communities have been sacrificed for corporate interests. The pending mega-merger between Canadian Pacific (CP) and Kansas City Southern (KCS) would be the latest blow to our already hardest-hit communities.

The Harris County region is one of only two non-attainment areas along the project's pathway across the U.S. and Mexico. Houston families are already dealing with the life-and-death consequences of another railway merger where the safety of our community was an afterthought.

We deserve a chance to voice our concerns about our families health, safety, and well-being.

support Commissioner Rodney Ellis’s effort for a public hearing in Houston.
